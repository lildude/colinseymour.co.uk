--- layout: post title: HG Wells Loves "tumultuous" date: 2012-03-20 04:21:58.000000000 -07:00 tags: [] type: post published: true ---

I finished reading The War of the Worlds by HG Wells last night and I must say, for a book first published in 1898, this guy had incredible foresight and an amazing imagination. Pity it didn't extend to his use of the word "tumultuous". I've never seen a book with quite so many uses of the word. That said, he does have a way with adjectives which is quite refreshing compared to many modern writers.

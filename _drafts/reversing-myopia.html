Claire forwarded me a very interesting article about reversing myopia without surgery or lenses or anything. All it ended is to actually train you eyes like you would you body.

Being one to try things to improve myself, I thought I'd give it a go starting today, 19 Aug, and see how I get on. The idea is to spend most of my working day without my specs on or contact lenses in and see if I notice any improvements over a month or two.

One thing is for certain, I do think there's something in this... this morning I was contact free until I needed to go out for my run. I put in my contact lenses and instantly found it hard to read my laptop screen. Seeing far with the lenses was fine,but close up was really hard work.

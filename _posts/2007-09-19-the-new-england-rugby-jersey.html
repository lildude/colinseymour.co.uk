Word is out that England have changed their rugby jersey AGAIN. As is common practice in the Internet age, photos of the new kit have slipped onto the net before they've been officially announced.

I think this is a much more appropriate design. The old O2 was more befitting of the English football team. The new O36 is much more appropriate for the soon-to-be ex-rugby world cup champs. ;-)

Google recently purchased a web site traffic analysis company, Urchin, and have now taken on their old "analytics" features, got rid of the $199 price tag and are now offering this service for free under the name "Google Analytics". To quote the website "Google Analytics tells you everything you want to know about how your visitors found you and how they interact with your site". I've just activated this on my site, so should hopefully have some cool stats to play with soon.

Before heading over to France to play a bit of French club rugby (and relinquish his chances of being selected for the Boks for a while), Francois Steyn built himself quite a reputation for being a big kicker who could easily slot the ball between the opposition's posts from within his own half, but check out this drop goal, it's amazing...

At a guess I'd say he easily had an extra 20 to 30m on that kick so a 90m kick is certainly in sight for Francois. Lets just hope he returns to South Africa before September next year or SARU relax their selection criteria.

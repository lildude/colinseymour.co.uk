The guys at Aardman, specifically the Sumo Science chappies, have come up with another great animation: Gulp

This is a stop motion video filmed at Pendine Beach in South Wales on a set covering 11 000 square feet using a crane, a lot of helping hands and three Nokia N8 mobile phones.

Pretty impressive stuff. If you're curious about the making of this video, be sure to click the "Watch the Making Of Here" text towards the end of the video.

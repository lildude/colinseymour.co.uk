I felt a rumbling in the flat at about 6:05 this morning as I ate my toast and drank my tea, and it was'nt my bowels. I got to work and almost everyone else noticed it too. BBC News soon revealed the cause of the rumbling - 3 explosions at an oil depot in Hemel Hempstead. Best fill up now before the price of petrol goes up again.

Following our snowfall on Saturday night, a couple of kids built a snowman on the grass across the road from us. Well, it's now Thursday and we've had 3 days of glorious sun shine and remnants of our neighbourhood snowman are still there...

He's lost his head, and his stature is a bit diminished, but he's not giving in... he just will not die like all other snowmen. He's a real fighter.

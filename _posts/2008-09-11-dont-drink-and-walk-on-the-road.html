The South African alcohol awareness peeps are getting a little over protective. In fact, more protective than the UK peeps for a change.

Mum bought a new box of wine last night and found this printed, yes printed, on the side of the box:

So, if you're going to drink, remember NO walking on the road. By all means, hop, skip, jump, run or even DRIVE, but just don't walk - you might be killed.

You have been warned.

Thanks Mum.

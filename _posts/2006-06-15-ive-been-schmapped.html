I received an email a couple of months ago from a guy at Schmap saying that he'd found several of the pictures I took when we went to Bath on Flickr, and would like to know if I was happy for them to be included in their guide for Bath.

I'm a nice guy, so I agreed - I'm not some professional photographer trying to make a buck off every photo I take after all.

Well, I received an email yesterday to say 5 of my pictures had been included in the Bath Schmap guide. Cool!!

So, if you're running Windows, download the player and see if you can find my pictures.

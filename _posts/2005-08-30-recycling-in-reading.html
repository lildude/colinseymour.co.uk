Well, Reading Borough Council have a unique way of recycling stuff - just enter "reading borough recycling opening hours" (without the quotes) into Google and hit "I'm Feeling Lucky" - now you can recycle all your crappy old computer equipment and be rest assured it will be taken care of. You can even pop in 365 days a year to lay flowers beside the final resting place.

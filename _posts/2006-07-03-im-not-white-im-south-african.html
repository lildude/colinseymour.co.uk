southafrica.info has an interesting article on how most South Africans view themselves as neither, black, white, pink nor khaki, but rather as "South African", when asked to define their primary identity - that is, the group to which they belong "first and foremost". I couldn't agree more, I'm not white, I'm South African, now all we need to do is get the rest of the world to add this option.

Tetleys have just released a new product - Redbush & Vanilla tea. It's quite an interesting take on the classic South African rooibos tea and it's quite tasty too. What is interesting, is their website calls it "Rooibos & Vanilla", but their advertising and the actual tin call it "Redbush & Vanilla" - I wonder why the change.

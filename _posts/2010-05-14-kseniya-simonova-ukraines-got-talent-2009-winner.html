How's this for an entry to a talent show...

This is the brilliant work of Kseniya Simonova, a 24 year old Ukrainian who won last year's "Ukraine's Got Talent" TV competition with this. The video shows Kseniya drawing a series of pictures on an illuminated sand table showing how ordinary people were affected by the German invasion during World War II. This is pretty impressive and moving stuff and definitely worthy of the top prize.

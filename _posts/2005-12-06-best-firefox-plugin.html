Chrome Cleaner has got to be the best Firefox Plugin I've installed. It speeds up Firefox's launch time by about a factor of 5 by removing duplicate entries from the installed-chrome.txt and references to non-existant jar files, and it works on Windows, *NIX and Mac. Now my Mac Mini launches Firefox after only one icon bounce instead of 5 or 6.

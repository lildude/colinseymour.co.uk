Who'd have thought it... the very very terrible Friday by Rebecca Black has been parodied by comedy duo 'Derick Watts and the Sunday Blues' in preparation for South Africa's National Braai day, held 24 September every year...

The tune is still terrible, but the lyrics are waaaaaay better.

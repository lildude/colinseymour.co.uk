Everyone is well aware of the terrorism plot foiled by the police the other day, but I've just found out that one of the internet cafes raided, after the event as part of evidence gathering, is just down the road from us. Claire and I actually walked right past this place on Thursday evening. Looks like someone has been a naughty boy on their computers.

Wow!!!! Usain Bolt is incredible, check that margin of victory, and he wasn't even trying in the last 10m. Usain Bolt won this race at 90m, cruised across the finish line and still SMASHED his old record to pieces in a new time of 9.69 seconds. Imagine how quick he would have been had he tried right to the end. This man definitely deserves the title of the world's fastest man. If you've not seen it, check it out on the BBC website.

Oh, and did anyone else notice that 6 of the 8 runners were from Caribbean Islands? Must be something in the water ;-)

The New Zealand police have setup a wiki allowing the the average Joe to help craft a new version of their Police Act. I think this is soooo cool. This is probably the most democratic move a government could ever do - give their citizens a voice that they can actually voice themselves, and know that it'll be read and considered.

If you are just a little bit curious about the New Zealand Police Act, or even think you've got something to contribute, head on over and check out the wiki: http://wiki.policeact.govt.nz/

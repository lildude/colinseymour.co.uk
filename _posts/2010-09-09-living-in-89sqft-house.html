Ever thought about living in a teeny weeny quaint little log cabin that you could take everywhere you went? No fixed address, but all your luxuries and possessions with you without actually living in a caravan? Well, maybe you need a Tumbleweed Tiny House.

I quite like the idea of building and living in a wooden house like those you get in the US or Scandinavia and have seen a lot of "Ooooooo aaaaahhh" wooden houses on Grand Designs, but I'm a bit too boisterous for such a teeny weeny house. I need something on a much grander scale. Nice idea though.

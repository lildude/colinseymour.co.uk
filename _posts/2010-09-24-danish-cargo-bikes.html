How's this for a great modification to the humble bicycle to extend its use?

I've seen, and quite like the idea of, bicycle trailers for towing your kids or shopping (you can borrow one from Waitrose if you don't have your own), but the Danes' cargo bikes take things to a whole new level.

Not sure I'll be trading in the car just yet though.

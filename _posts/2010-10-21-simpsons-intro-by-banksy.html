I'm sure every fan of the Simpsons eagerly watches the start of each episode to see how it differs from the others. Well, keep you eye out for this episode that aired on Sunday in the US...

If you're not quite sure what you're looking at, it's the work of the well known British graffiti street artist Banksy. Apparently he storyboarded and directed the opening sequence.

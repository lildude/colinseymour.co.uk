A while back I mentioned I ordered a new car, well it arrived yesterday and I was like a child on Christmas morning, except without all the sweeties and wrapping paper.

As you may know, I'm a bit of a fan of Volkswagen, and chose to stick with VW for this car. I've always liked the GTi ever since I had one as a company car, however it's expensive, not the most economical car in the world, and insuring it would be a nightmare. So instead I've gone for the Golf GT TSI 170PS.

The GT quenches my desire for a fast and nippy car that doesn't break the bank, and manages to keep costs down as it's very economical too, thanks to it's 1.4 litre engine.

I know, I know... you're probably thinking "1.4 litres!!!??? My lawnmower has got a bigger engine than that", and I thought that too until I test drove the GT. Boy is this baby quick.

Some quick facts to help put this into perspective:

  • Max Power Output: 168 bhp / 170 PS
  • Top Speed: 136mph / 220km/h
  • 0 - 62mph/100km/h: 7.9 sec
  • Economy:
    • Urban: 28.5mpg - 9.9l/100km
    • Extra-urban: 48.7mpg - 5.8l/100km
    • Combined: 38.7mpg - 7.3l/100km

It's quicker than the Mark IV Golf GTi and more economical than 1.6 Golf I've just passed onto Claire. Pretty impressive for a 1.4 litre engine. If you're interested in how this is all possible, check this article.

Yes folks, today, 18 July 2010, is the first international Mandela Day to mark the contribution of Nelson Mandela to world freedom. South Africa has celebrated Mandela Day for while, but last year the UN gave it a thumbs up on the international calendar. What would have been brilliant is if the UN had made it a mandatory bank holiday too.

The redesigned Springbok jersey has been revealed (you'll get to see it on the advert on Supersport this evening if you live in SA) and can be seen as a harmonious agreement between one of South Africa's oldest icons, the springbok and the new, the protea.

As you can see from this fetching photo of John Smit, the redesign sees the jumping springbok move to the right of the jersey, the Canterbury logo to the middle and the national sports emblem, the King Protea, on the left.

A lot of die-hard rugby fans aren't going to be too happy with this, but I think it's a very happy compromise that keeps everyone happy. It also shows brings rugby inline with the other major South African sports, like...

... the football... ... and cricket... ... and the test cricket shirt ...

Hmmm, I don't think the cricket peeps thought this one through.

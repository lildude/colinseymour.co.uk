Today is Groundhog Day, and Punxsutawney Phil popped out, saw his shadow and declared we'd have 6 more weeks of winter. Well, at least those in the US will. I think those of us in the south of England may see summer come a bit sooner as the daffs are already starting to flower and we've had two lovely sunny days whilst our northern friends battled with ran, snow and gale force winds.

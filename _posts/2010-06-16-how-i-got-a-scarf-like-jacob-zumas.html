I happened comment to my mum about how I liked the scarf Jacob Zuma was wearing at the opening ceremony of the football World Cup...

Mum, then took it upon herself to see if she could source one for herself and me. After a lot of trawling on the internet, she gave up and decided to email the President instead...

From: Seymour
To: The President Subject: Help please
Date: Wed, 16 Jun 2010 11:10:19 +0200

TO WHOM IT MAY CONCERN

Please advise where I may purchase a scarf like the one worn by
President Zuma at the World Cup opening game.

Unfortunately I am currently house-bound following a serious motor
vehicle accident, but would love to purchase one for myself and one for
my son, who is currently living in England.

Trusting that you will be able to assist with my query.

Many thanks.

Regards

Helen Seymour

Obviously, this was a shot in the dark, but there's no harm asking.

Well blow me down with a feather. My mum actually got a reply, and a useful one at that...

Coming

Now how's that for a responsive presidential office of a third world president? I'm impressed.

As luck would have it, it turns out they're being sold on the side of the road all over South Africa, but of course mum wouldn't know this being confined to the house. :-)

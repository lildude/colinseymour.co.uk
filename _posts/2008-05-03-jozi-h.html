Tonight I was flipping through the channels on our Sky box (I don't subscribe, I just see what I can get for free) and stumbled upon "Urban TV" showing a dreadful South African drama - "Jozi - H". It's apparently a medical drama set in "Johannesburg's trauma unit" in a kind of ER style. One question: Which one? Jo'burg has many, and I certainly don't recognise this one.

Come to think of it, I can't say I've ever heard of a TV program in SA called "Jozi - H" (the first SA program made purely for a foreign market?), but one thing is for sure, it's strange to see a TV program where the American accent is put on, and not the South African.

Daisy’s Dream supports bereaved children and their families throughout Berkshire and the surrounding areas. They’ve put themselves forward as a potential recipient of funding from Natwest’s Twyford Branch. If they receive the most votes, they will be awarded £3000.

You can vote in the branch or online - https://communityfund.natwest.com/Vote/Details. If you vote online please enter the postcode of RG10 9EU and select the Twyford branch to bring up Daisy’s Dream in the selection.

You can find out more about Daisy’s Dream here: http://www.daisysdream.org.uk/

Today is the official Left Handers Day 2005

Take a moment and have a thought for all the lefties (left handed people) in your life. Think about all the right handed items they're forced to use everyday because no left handed alternative exist or because the left handed alternative is just too expensive, and take sympathy just this one day of the year.
Then head on over to the official Left Handers Day website and read up on some interesting facts about lefties.

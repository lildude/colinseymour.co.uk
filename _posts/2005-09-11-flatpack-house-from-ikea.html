Swedish living is taking over in the UK. IKEA is now offering flatpack housing... well, almost. You can't pop into IKEA and pick up a box and take it home, but almost. IKEA is partnering with several firms to bring more cheap, affordable (wooden) housing to the UK - The BoKlok Concept.

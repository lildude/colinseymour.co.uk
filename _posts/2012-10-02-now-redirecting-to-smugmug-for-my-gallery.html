In my infinite wisdom, many years ago I decided I'd write my own code to pull my photos from SmugMug, using their API, and then display then in a nice looking gallery here. Well, not anymore. This has worked well for a while, but I've realised I'm re-inventing the wheel unnecessarily and this re-invention is proving a nightmare to maintain. So from now on, when you click the Gallery link in my header, you'll go to my public SmugMug page.

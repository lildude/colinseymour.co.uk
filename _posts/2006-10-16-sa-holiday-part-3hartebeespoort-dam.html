I've finally pulled finger and worked my way through my large collection of photos taken from our second week on holiday in South Africa.

We spent the week with mum and Sami, utilising mum's time share (again), staying at a resort called Mount Amanzi. This is situated in the beautiful and quite valley downstream from the Harties Dam wall.

It was a lovely week of R&R and included a spectacular visit to the De Wildt Cheetah Centre just down the road. If you're ever in the Northern Province round Harties direction, I highly recommend visiting De Wildt Cheetah Centre, and you must cough up for the photo with a cheetah.

You can check out all the pics in my gallery. Oh and if you're into ornithology, there are some really awesome bird pictures too, like this beaut I took of a Brown Hooded Kingfisher...

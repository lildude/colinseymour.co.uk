Kulula, one of SA's cheapo flight companies is campaigning for daylight savings time to be introduced in SA. Head over to their page and read all about it. Be sure to sign the petition (22374 votes already) - you may not live in SA, but just think what you could do with that extra hour when you go there on holiday.
